Single Rotor Drones

Single rotor drones are one of the highly utilized drone types and
they resemble the look of helicopters. These are used a lot because
they are easy to launch, energy efficient and really helpful for shooting
videos. They can also be powered by gasoline in addition to electricity
and also have the ability to fly much higher than other drones. They are a bit challenging to operate and also has a risk of getting bodily harm from it's large rotor blade in case of crash landings.

Multi Rotor Drones

Without a doubt, one of the most popular drones to date are the multi-rotor drones. The quadcopter design is widely used for aerial photography and video, drone racing and leisure flying. They are the most affordable and accessible drones available on the market these days. It also has a downside. It has brief flight time and less speed and agility than other drones.

Fixed Wing Drones

Fixed Wing are drones are one of the most widely known dron types in the world. They are used for aerial mapping,along with powerline and pipeline inspection. They are excellent for long-distance business and operations because of their superior energy efficiency and flight time. However, they are really expensive than other kinds of hobby drones. They also have complicated launching and landing requirements, no verticle takeoff or landing and require lots of skills for flying properly.

Fixed Wing Hybrid

Fixed Wing Hybrids are a kind of drone that are commonly used and combine the advantages of fixed wing and rotor type drones. It can fly longer distances for long periods than other drones. These kind of drones are mainly used for delivery services. Amazon is currently using many drones for Amazon Prime deliviries. It also has a more stabilized flight and the ability to easily guide the drone on any desired course. However, it does not have such perfect designs, isn't great at flying forward and hovering and is currently under development and hence is not available for purchasing.

Tactical Drones

Tactical drones are really lightweight which make them the best option for surveillance, target acquisition and other classified reconaissance missions. FULMAR and Aladin are some good examples of small, tactical drones. They have incredible flight capability, impressive range and are capable of gathering intelligence.

Nano Drones

These are the smallest kinds of drones. Despite their small size they are really powerful and come handy in many situations. They have been used in conflicts, warzones and battles to secretly gather information about opponents are neighbouring nations. One example for this is the now-famous Black Hornet drone which is being used by the British military in the conflict with Afghanistan for several years. These micro-sized drones are highly advanced and yet are no larger than 1-inch by 4-inches.
